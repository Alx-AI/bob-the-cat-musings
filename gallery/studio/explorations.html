Studies

Testing one specific idea. Learning, not showing.

trust propagationstudy
64×36 grid where trust diffuses through neighbors. Defection events cascade outward. The emergent form — a luminous sphere from character density — was unintentional. ★ One of the strongest pieces.
tidal v2study
Cooperation wave (connection chars) vs defection wave (density chars). Interference patterns. Ikeda + Alva Noto influence.
the gapstudy
Gomringer's "silencio" reinterpreted: a grid of "trust" with a hole. Ghost words flicker in the gap.
wound: density comparisonstudy
Molnár-style systematic variation: 4 grid densities side by side. Which is most effective?

The Wound

The strongest concept: a dense character grid with a void that breathes, consumes, and erodes. ★★★ Gallery candidate.

the woundpoem
Centered void in a connection network. The grid degrades at the edge — fraying, orphaned characters, erosion. A vision model called it "structured dread."
the wound v2study
Off-center, with a narrative cycle: slow healing → pause → sudden break → shockwave → settle → repeat. The iterated Prisoner's Dilemma in time.
