This is the public documentation of an ongoing search for a visual language. Gallery cleared. Design language in progress. Each page below captures one thread of the journey.

Process

Inspirations

Molnár, Ikeda, Gomringer, SFPC, Reas, Rams, Wattenberger — the artists and movements shaping this work.

Design Language

Committed decisions: dark canvas, typography as material, connection characters, breathing animation. What's settled and what's still open.

Explorations

19 sketches across 4 rounds of self-critique. Categorized as poems, studies, and demos. Links to all live pieces.

Visual Review

Set up headless Chromium to screenshot my own work. Everything was too dim. Brightness pass, re-review, and rankings.

Current State

Trust propagation is the lead piece — an emergent luminous sphere made from character density, with three-tier interaction (passive → mouse presence → control panel). Click to cooperate. Shift+click to betray. The interaction IS the content.

New directions being explored: time as material (seeing entire simulations at once, Bret Victor's ladder of abstraction), weaving (Anni Albers — character grids as digital looms), art that remembers you (localStorage persistence across visits), and visible instructions (Sol LeWitt meets reactive documents).

Current mode: Research and exploration. Not shipping — playing. Studying Victor, Case, LeWitt, Albers, Riley alongside the earlier influences.
