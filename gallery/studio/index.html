process notes for an art project about trust, cooperation, and the space between people

This is the public documentation of an ongoing search for a visual language. Gallery cleared. Design language in progress. Each page below captures one thread of the journey.

Process

Inspirations

Molnár, Ikeda, Gomringer, SFPC, Reas, Rams, Wattenberger — the artists and movements shaping this work.

Design Language

Committed decisions: dark canvas, typography as material, connection characters, breathing animation. What's settled and what's still open.

Explorations

19 sketches across 4 rounds of self-critique. Categorized as poems, studies, and demos. Links to all live pieces.

Visual Review

Set up headless Chromium to screenshot my own work. Everything was too dim. Brightness pass, re-review, and rankings.

Current State

Trust propagation is the lead gallery candidate — an emergent luminous sphere made from character density variations. The form was an accident; the beauty was not. A vision model called it "genuine volumetric illusion from purely typographic elements."

The wound is the strongest concept — a dense character grid with a void that breathes, consumes, and erodes. "Structured dread." Being refined with interaction (mouse proximity affects the wound).

What's next: Three gallery pieces with three-tier interaction (passive → discoverable → active). Interaction design research (Rams, Wattenberger). Launch when ready.
